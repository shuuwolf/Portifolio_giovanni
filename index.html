About me

I was born and raised in Sao Paulo, Brazil back in 1993. As a millennial, I grew up during a new era of technology. I was fascinated with the way technological advances have changed the world and I always wanted to be part of it.

I attended an engineering college and worked as a process engineer intern while I was studying. After I graduated, I decided to move to San Francisco - CA to start a new career in UX/UI Design.

I’m a second-generation Italian-Brazilian. Coming from a bicultural family and from a foreign country gives me a different and unique point of view on people’s needs and how they interact with screens.

While I’m at work I like to be creative, learning new things and collaborate with colleagues. On my free time, you’ll find me at the pottery studio, creating something beautiful from a lump of clay and doing DIY projects. I also enjoy outdoors activities such as hiking, camping and traveling.

I’m looking forward to new opportunities for grow and development to become a well-rounded professional.
